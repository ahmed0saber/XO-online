About the game

Online Tic Tac Toe is a web game where you can play 4 different modes

•play vs easy bot or a random-move bot

•play vs AI bot or unbeatable bot that works with artificial intelligence using minimax algorithm

•play vs a person locally , from one device

•play vs a person online , from 2 different devices
